Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin, generally consisting of cheese and a tomato sauce on top of flat bread.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni Pizza

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Combine first four ingredients with water and mix into dough
  3. Pound flat and top with tomato sauce and garlic
  4. Sprinkle with cheese and pepperoni
  5. Cook in oven for 20 minutes
